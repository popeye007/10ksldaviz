We conduct business outside the United States which exposes us to foreign currency, import restrictions, taxes, duties and other risks. We depend on independent manufacturers to produce the majority of our products and our business could suffer if we need to replace manufacturers or suppliers or find additional capacity. The loss of one or more of our major suppliers for materials may interrupt our supplies. Our business could be adversely impacted by any disruption to our supply chain. Our business could be adversely impacted by the financial instability of our customers.
