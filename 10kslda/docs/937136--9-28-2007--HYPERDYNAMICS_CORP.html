WE MAY HAVE WRITE DOWNS OF OUR ASSETS DUE TO PRICE VOLATILITY. ESTIMATES OF OIL AND GAS RESERVES ARE UNCERTAIN AND MAY VARY SUBSTANTIALLY FROM ACTUAL PRODUCTION. THE UNAVAILABILITY OR HIGH COST OF DRILLING RIGS, EQUIPMENT, SUPPLIES, PERSONNEL AND OILFIELD SERVICES COULD ADVERSELY IMPACT US. FAILURE TO FIND OIL AND GAS.
