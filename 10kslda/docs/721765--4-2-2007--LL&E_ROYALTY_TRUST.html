Increased production and development costs for the Overriding Royalties will result in decreased Trust distributions. Trust reserve estimates depend on many assumptions that may prove to be inaccurate, which could cause both estimated reserves and estimated future revenues to be too high. Operating risks can adversely affect Trust distributions. The operators of the properties are subject to extensive governmental regulation. Trust Unit holders and the Trustee have no control over the operation or development of the Royalty Properties and have little influence over operation or development. The owner of any Properties may abandon any property, terminating the related Overriding Royalties. The Trust can be terminated and may terminate in 2007. Trust assets are depleting assets and, if the operators of the properties do not perform additional development projects, the assets may deplete faster than expected.
