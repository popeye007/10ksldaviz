Eventually, the assets of the Trust will cease to produce in commercial quantities and the Trust will cease to receive proceeds from such assets. Unitholders and the Trustee have no influence over the operations on, or future development of, the Underlying Properties. The Company may transfer its interest in any Underlying Property without the consent of the Trust or the Unitholders.
