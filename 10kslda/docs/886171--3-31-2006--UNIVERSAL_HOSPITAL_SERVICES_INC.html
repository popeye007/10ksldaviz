Our competitors may engage in significant price competition or liquidate significant amounts of surplus equipment, thereby decreasing the demand for outsourcing services and possibly causing us to reduce the rates we charge for our services. We have relationships with certain key suppliers, and adverse developments concerning these suppliers could delay our ability to procure equipment or increase our cost of purchasing equipment. A portion of our revenues are derived from home care providers and nursing homes, and these health care providers may pose additional credit risks. Our customers operate in a highly regulated environment and the regulations affecting them could lead to additional expenses associated with compliance and licensing, along with penalties resulting from possible violations, thereby increasing our costs and reducing income. We may incur increased vendor costs that we cannot pass through to our customers.
