As a result, our financial results are dependent on the economic growth and strength of those areas. Our customers may be unwilling or unable to meet lot takedown commitments due to liquidity limitations or slowing market conditions. Risks Related to our Mineral Resources Operations We cannot control activities on oil or gas properties we do not operate. General Risks Related to our Operations Both our real estate and mineral resources businesses are cyclical in nature.
