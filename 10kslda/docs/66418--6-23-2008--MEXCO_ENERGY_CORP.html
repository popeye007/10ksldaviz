Volatility of oil and gas prices significantly affects our results and profitability. Lower oil and gas prices and other factors may cause us to record ceiling test writedowns. Information concerning our reserves and future net revenues estimates is inherently uncertain. We must replace reserves we produce. Acquisitions are subject to the risks and uncertainties of evaluating reserves and potential liabilities and may be disruptive and difficult to integrate into our business. Drilling and operating activities are high risk activities that subject us to a variety of factors that we can not control. Our business depends on oil and natural gas transportation facilities which are owned by others. We may not be insured against all of the operating hazards to which our business is exposed. The oil and gas industry is highly competitive. Our business is subject to extensive environmental regulations, and to laws that can give rise to liabilities from environmental contamination.
