We may be unable to compete effectively against other airlines with greater financial resources or lower operating costs, or to adjust rapidly enough in the event the nature of competition in our markets changes. Our business, financial condition, and results of operations are substantially exposed to the current high prices and variability of jet fuel. Further increases in jet fuel costs would harm our business. A significant increase in labor costs or change in key personnel could adversely affect our business and results of operations. Our failure to successfully reduce unit costs at both Alaska and Horizon could harm our business. Our operations are often affected by factors beyond our control, including changing economic and other conditions, which could harm our financial condition and results of operations. We are dependent on a limited number of suppliers for aircraft and parts.
