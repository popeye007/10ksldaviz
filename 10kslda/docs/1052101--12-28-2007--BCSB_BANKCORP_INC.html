Failure to comply with the Supervisory Agreement could result in additional enforcement action against it, including the imposition of monetary penalties, and could adversely affect the Bank s ability to meet the obligations under the Company s trust preferred securities. The Bank has had losses and low earnings in recent years, and if it cannot generate and increase its income to competitive levels the Company s stock price may be adversely affected. The Bank and Company operate in a highly regulated environment and may be adversely affected by changes in laws and regulations.
