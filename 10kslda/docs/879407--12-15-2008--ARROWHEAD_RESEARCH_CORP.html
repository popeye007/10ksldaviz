The costs and effect of consolidating Unidym s facilities and operations are difficult to predict and could be substantial. We have not generated significant revenues and our business model does not predict significant revenues in the foreseeable future. We will need to achieve commercial acceptance of our applications to generate revenues and achieve profitability. We may be unable to scale up our manufacturing processes in a cost effective way. Nanotechnology-enabled products are new and may be viewed as being harmful to human health or the environment. There may be a difference in the investment valuations that we used when making initial and subsequent investments in our Subsidiaries and Minority Investments and actual market values.
