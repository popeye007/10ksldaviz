OUR RESERVES COULD BE INADEQUATE DUE TO DIFFERENCES BETWEEN OUR ACTUAL EXPERIENCE AND MANAGEMENT'S ESTIMATES AND ASSUMPTIONS. OUR EARNINGS ARE IMPACTED BY DAC AND VOBA CALCULATIONS THAT ARE BASED ON ESTIMATES THAT ARE SUBJECT TO CHANGE. A DOWNGRADE IN OUR FINANCIAL STRENGTH AND CLAIMS-PAYING RATINGS COULD ADVERSELY AFFECT OUR BUSINESS AND RESULTS OF OPERATIONS. LEGAL AND REGULATORY ACTIONS COULD HAVE A MATERIAL ADVERSE AFFECT ON OUR BUSINESSES. OUR BUSINESSES MAY BE ADVERSELY AFFECTED TO THE EXTENT THAT WE, THIRD-PARTY FIRMS THAT DISTRIBUTE OUR PRODUCTS OR UNAFFILIATED INSURERS FACE INCREASED REGULATION AND/OR HEIGHTENED REGULATORY SCRUTINY. CHANGES IN U.S. TAX LAWS MAY ADVERSELY AFFECT SALES OF OUR PRODUCTS AND OUR PROFITABILITY. WE FACE INTENSE COMPETITION FROM OTHER INSURANCE COMPANIES, BANKS AND OTHER FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS, WHICH MAY ADVERSELY IMPACT OUR MARKET SHARE AND PROFITABILITY. CHANGES IN STATUTORY RESERVE REQUIREMENTS AND ADVERSE MARKET CONDITIONS COULD NEGATIVELY IMPACT OUR SALES. CHANGES IN ACCOUNTING STANDARDS COULD HAVE A MATERIAL ADVERSE EFFECT ON OUR RESULTS OF OPERATIONS AND/OR FINANCIAL POSITION. OUR LOSSES PROVIDED FOR DISCONTINUED OPERATIONS MAY DIFFER FROM THE LOSSES ULTIMATELY REALIZED. OUR DISCLOSURE AND INTERNAL CONTROL SYSTEM CANNOT GUARANTEE THAT OUR PUBLIC DISCLOSURE AND FINANCIAL STATEMENTS DO NOT CONTAIN ERRORS.
