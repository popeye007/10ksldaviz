Risks Related to Our Business We could experience a decrease in the demand for our products resulting in lower sales volumes, which would give us less capital with which to operate. Production time and the overall cost of our products could increase resulting in lower profit margins for our products if any of our primary suppliers are lost or if any of them increased their prices of raw materials. We could lose our credit agreement and could result in our not being able to pay our creditors.
