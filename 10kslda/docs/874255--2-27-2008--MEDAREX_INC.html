Clinical trials required for our product candidates are expensive and time-consuming, and their outcome is uncertain. Success in early clinical trials may not be indicative of results obtained in later trials. Products employing our antibody technology may fail to gain market acceptance. The successful commercialization of our antibody products will depend on obtaining coverage and reimbursement for use of these products from third-party payors. The continuing efforts of governmental and third-party payers to contain or reduce the costs of healthcare may impair our future revenues and profitability.
