Market prices of wholesale shell eggs are volatile and changes in these prices and costs can adversely impact our results of operations. Changes in consumer demand for shell eggs can negatively impact our business. Feed costs are volatile and changes in these costs can adversely impact our results of operations. Due to the cyclical nature of our business, our financial results from year to year may fluctuate. We purchase approximately 22% of the shell eggs we sell from outside producers and our ability to obtain such eggs at prices and in quantities acceptable to us could fluctuate. Our acquisition growth strategy subjects us to various risks. Our largest customers have historically accounted for a significant portion of our net sales volume. Accordingly, our business may be adversely affected by the loss of, or reduced purchases by, one or more of our large customers. Our business is highly competitive.
