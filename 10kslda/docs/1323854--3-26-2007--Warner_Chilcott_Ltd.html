Special Note Regarding Forward-Looking Statements Risks Relating to Our Business If generic products that compete with any of our branded pharmaceutical products are approved, sales of our products may be adversely affected. The loss of the services of members of our senior management team or scientific staff or the inability to attract and retain other highly qualified employees could impede our ability to meet our strategic objectives and adversely affect our business. Product liability claims and product recalls could harm our business. Sales of our products may be adversely affected by the consolidation among wholesale drug distributors and the growth of large retail drug store chains. If we fail to comply with government regulations we could be subject to fines, sanctions and penalties that could adversely affect our ability to operate our business. The terms of our senior secured credit facility and the indenture governing the Notes restrict our current and future operations, particularly our ability to respond to changes in our business or to take certain actions. Risks Relating to Our Common Stock Future sales of our shares could depress the market price of our common stock. The market price of our common stock may be volatile. Provisions of our bye-laws could delay or prevent a takeover of us by a third party.
